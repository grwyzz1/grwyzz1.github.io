Opus 2

The project aims to expose the problems of excessive consumption and environmental pollution that lie behind the fervour and festivity of the Spring Festival. The source of the problem is fireworks. By producing AR posters that more directly show the pollution of rivers and land by waste and chemicals from fireworks, viewers are forced to reflect on their actions and focus on these often-overlooked issues. The work is more storytelling-like using illustration, and the poster designs for the films fireworks and parasite provide inspiration for the work's presentation style, namely composition and critical perspective. Using AR technology, certain elements of the poster will be displayed dynamically, such as rubbish from fireworks and chemical pollution in the river.

The project uses PS, AI, AE and Eyejack software to create and improve AR posters.

The design process of this work includes making and adjusting the static part with PS and Ai first, then realizing the dynamic effect through AE, finally synthesizing AR effect with Eyejack. I hope that through this poster, the audience can resonate, and start to pay attention to and take a more environmentally friendly way to make the festival on the basis of environmental protection.
