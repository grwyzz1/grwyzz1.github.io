Project Overview: "Reality" after the Spring Festival Project Background: Spring Festival, the most distinctive traditional festival in the East, is a time of celebration. However, behind the enthusiasm and festive nature of this festival are the problems of overconsumption and environmental pollution, especially caused by the setting off of fireworks.
Project Goal: To create an AR poster that aims to critically reveal the consumption and environmental issues behind the celebration of the Chinese New Year, prompting viewers to reflect on their own behavior and focus on these often overlooked issues.
Content description:
Design the poster through the illustration style to make it more "storytelling" feeling.
The posters for the films Fireworks and Parasite provided the inspiration for the design, with the former providing the festive backdrop and the latter adding a critical perspective to the poster. The poster will show the festive excitement and celebration, but at the same time, it will also reveal the waste and environmental pollution caused by the celebration.
Using AR technology, certain elements in the poster will be dynamic, such as garbage from fireworks and chemical pollution in rivers.
Technical application:
Use four software: PS, AI, AE, and Eyejack to create and refine AR posters.
The specific process involves making and adjusting the static part with PS and AI, then achieving dynamic effects through AE, and finally using Eyejack to synthesize AR effects.
Desired effect: I hope that through this poster, it will resonate with the audience, make them aware of the environmental problems that may be brought about by the celebration, so as to reflect on their own behavior and adopt a more environmentally friendly way to celebrate the holiday.
