About Me

I studied Creative Industries at Queensland University of Technology and my majors were in Entertainment and Visual Communication. I am a positive, optimistic and studious person. Through three years of university study, I not only have a preliminary understanding of the work content and development potential of the entertainment industry, but also proficiently apply AI, PS, ID and other drawing software and Word, PPT, excel and other office software.

My passion for the profession stems not only from my curiosity about the entertainment industry, but also from my interest in art and culture. I believe that art and entertainment is not only a way to relax, but also a way to express and transmit emotions, a link between culture and audience. I hope that through my portfolio, I can convey my dedication and love for the culture, art and entertainment industry.

Thank you for browsing my personal portfolio, I will continue to strive to continue to explore and learn in the industry. If you need any help or any suggestions, you are welcome to contact me at any time.
