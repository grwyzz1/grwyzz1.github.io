This work is for the pet coffee shop brand image design Logo design has gradually become the core of the brand, high identification of the brand, the possibility of its selection will be greater. Therefore, the goal of this design is to combine the pet and coffee elements, for the pet cafe logo design and suitable for different occasions of the poster. It is hoped that while promoting coffee and pet culture, target users can be cured by drinking coffee and interacting with pets at the same time. The design style is concise, allowing customers to receive information directly. At the same time for the coffee brand to establish a good image, enhance cultural exchanges while building recognition. After researching the signs of existing pet coffee shops, I found that most of the signs used simple lines to combine pet and coffee elements, which gave me great inspiration.

Given the fact that customers may get tired of having too many similar coffee elements in their posters, I have highlighted uniqueness in the design.

1. Use life scenarios to appeal to customers' empathy and interest. For example, personifying a puppy at work, at home, or as a delivery person becomes a metaphor for the target customer's role in the city.

2. Considering summer, use bright colors to create a visually relaxing atmosphere, such as swimming and sunbathing puppies to recreate the normal behavior of customers at the beach, increasing the sense of expectation.

3. Use the correct knowledge of playing with pets and touching dogs to stimulate the target customers from the basis of pet love to further stimulate customers' patronage and consumption desire.

The brand image design of this series not only blends coffee and pet elements perfectly, but also conveys the core value of the brand: in the fast-paced life of the city, provide a space for customers to interact and heal with their pets.
