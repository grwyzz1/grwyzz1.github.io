Synopsis: Pet coffee shop brand image design
With the strengthening of brand awareness, logo design has become the core of sales. The more recognizable a brand is, the greater the probability of it being chosen. The logo not only directly reflects the taste, style and quality of the product, but also serves as a bridge between enterprises and consumers.
Target:
Promote coffee and pet culture by combining coffee and cute pet elements.
Let customers enjoy coffee while being healed by interacting with their pets.
Integrate the theme of the coffee shop in a simple way so that customers can receive information directly.
Establish a good image for the coffee brand, strengthen cultural exchange, and build recognition. Design strategy:
Logo Design: Through my research on existing pet coffee shop logos, I found that most logos use simple lines to combine pet and coffee elements, which gives me great inspiration.
Poster Design:
Considering that customers can have aesthetic fatigue with too many similar posters, I highlighted the uniqueness when designing.
Adopting a modern style, using a sense of humor and cultural references, the poster creates micro-life scenes in the city for customers. For example, personifying a puppy at work, at home, or as a delivery man becomes a metaphor for the target customer's role in the city.
With summer in mind, the use of bright colors to create a visually relaxing atmosphere such as swimming and sunbathing puppies reproduces the normal behavior of customers at the beach, increasing the sense of expectation.
Use the correct knowledge of playing with pets and touching dogs to stimulate target customers to further stimulate customers' desire to patronize and consume from liking pets.
Conclusion: This series of brand identity design not only perfectly integrates coffee and pet elements, but also conveys the core value of the brand: to provide customers with a space where they can relax, interact with pets and heal in the fast-paced life of the city.
